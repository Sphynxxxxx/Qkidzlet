Recognizing Nouns

Objectives

• Define nouns.
• Recognize nouns in simple phrases and sentences.

WHAT ARE NOUNS?

Nouns are the names that we give to a person, place, or thing.

Examples:

Doctor is a person.

Other nouns for person/people are:
girl, boy, sister, brother.

A school is a place.

Other nouns for places are:
beach, hospital, house, market.

A bag is a thing.

Other nouns for things are:
pencil, laptop, crayon, notebook.



Singular and Plural

Objectives

• Distinguish the difference between singular and plural nouns.
• Create plural words by adding –s and –es.

Singular Nouns

Singular Nouns are names that we use for a single person, place, or thing.


Plural Nouns

Plural nouns are names that we use for more than one person, place, or thing. Plurals are formed by adding ‘s’, ‘es’, and ‘ies’ at the end of the word.
